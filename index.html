Travel Notes

  • Book Forbidden City & Palace Museum tickets online well in advance — they sell out, and the museum is closed on Mondays.
  • Book Shanghai Astronomy Museum tickets online in advance — they also sell out quickly.
  • Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum in Nanjing requires free pre-booking via WeChat.
  • For the Great Wall, Mutianyu has fewer crowds than Badaling and has a toboggan ride down.
  • All intercity legs by high-speed rail: Shanghai–Suzhou (30 min), Suzhou–Hangzhou (1.5 hrs), Hangzhou–Nanjing (2 hrs), Nanjing–Beijing (3.5–4 hrs).
  • Qingshan Lake (26 May): go early for the best light on the metasequoia groves. Metro Line 16 → Qingshanhu Station → bus 846 one stop.
